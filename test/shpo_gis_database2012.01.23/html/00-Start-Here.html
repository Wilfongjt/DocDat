Access Points Definitions
(public)(pubic) refers to a public access point.
(gov)(gov) refers to a protected access point for reviewers, managers, approvers, help-desk, and system-administrators.

Privilege Definitions
(guest)(guest) is a role that gives minimum access to the public system. This level of access allows an actor to initiate an account.
(account)(account) is the first privilege granted. This level of access allows an actor to complete the registration.
(registrant)(registrant) has completed his or her registration and can request NRHP and ER privileges.
(NRHP)(NRHP) allow an actor to initiate new National Register of Historic Places Preliminary Questionnaire or Nomination
(ER)(ER) allow an actor to initiate new Section 106 Reviews
(editor)(editor) gives an actor access to modify, and complete nominations and reviews. In addition, this role can invite other registrants to assist with the preparation process materials.
(editor-proxy)(editor-proxy) gives an actor access to modify a specific nomination or review.
(reviewer)(reviewer) gives access to read and make comments about a specific nomination or review.
