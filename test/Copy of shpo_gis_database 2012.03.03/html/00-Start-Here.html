Access Points Definitions
(public)(pubic) refers to a public access point.
(gov)(gov) is a protected access point for reviewers, managers, approvers, help-desk, and system-administrators..

Privilege Definitions
(guest)(guest) is a role that gives minimum access to the public system. This level of access allows an actor to initiate an account.
(account)(account) is the first privilege granted. This level of access allows an actor to complete the registration.
(registrant)(registrant) is the second privilege granted. This level of access allows an actor to request permissions to create and update NRNs or ERs.
(NRN)(NRN) is the National Register
(ER)
(editor)(editor) gives an actor access to initiate and complete processes. This role must be requested by an actor. In addition, this role can invite other registrants to assist with the preparation process materials.
(editor-proxy)(editor-proxy) gives an actor access to a specific process. This role is assigned by an (editor) via an invitation to work contribute to develoment of process materials.
(reviewer)(reviewer) gives read only access to review specific process information. (reviewer) has create, read access to prepare and reply to comments.
